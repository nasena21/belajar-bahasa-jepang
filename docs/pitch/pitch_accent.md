## Part of Speech

Part of Speech terbagi menjadi 12 berdasarkan apakah mereka bisa berdiri sendiri, dan apakah mereka bisa berkonjugasi. 

| Part of Speech      | Definisi                          | Bisa Berdiri Sendiri?  | Bisa dikonjugasikan?  |Contoh|
| :---------: | :----------------------------------: | :----------------------------------: |:----------------------------------:  |:----------------------------------:  |
| Verbs  (動詞)|A word that describes an action, state, or occurrence.  |〇|〇|走る|
| Adjectives (形容詞)| A word that describes an attribute.|〇|〇|早い|
| Adjectival Nouns ( 形容動詞)|A word that describes an attribute while also being noun-like.|〇|〇|簡単な|
| Nouns(名詞) |A word that describes a person, place, state, quality, event, or thing. |〇|X|猫|
| Pronouns (代名詞)	| A word that indirectly describes a person, direction, or thing.|〇|X|わたし|
| Numbers (数詞)|A word that counts or measures entities.  |〇|X|七|
| Adnominal Adjectives (連体詞)| A word that describes an attribute by directly modifying a noun.|〇|X|この|
| Adverbs (副詞)|A word that qualifies an adjective, adjectival noun, or a verb.  |〇|X|まだ|
| Conjunctions (接続詞)| A word that connects a sentence together.|〇|X|しかし|
| Interjections (感動詞)|A word that represents an abrupt/emotional remark/response.  |〇|X|はい|
| Auxiliary Verbs (助動詞)|An ending that attaches to a conjugatable part of speech.  |X|〇|～ない|
| Particles(助詞)|A word that marks some grammatical function.|X|X|が・は・を etc|
Source : [Imabi](https://imabi.org/what-is-japanese/)

Lalu dari data di atas dapat diambil kesimpulan bahwa dari dua belas part of speech, hanya ada empat yang bisa dikonjugasikan

1. Verb

2. Adjective

3. Adjectival Nouns

4. Auxiliary Verbs

Yang dimana kalau kita sederhanakan lagi, maka hanya ada 2 part of speech yang bisa dikonjugasikan dalam bahasa Jepang

1. Verb
    - Godan Verbs
    - Ichidan Verbs
    - Irregular Verbs
    - Auxiliary Verbs

2. Adjective
    - I-adjectives
    - Adjectival Nouns (Na-adjectives)

!!! Note "Noun"

    Catatan untuk Noun, meskipun noun tidak dapat dikonjugasikan, noun bisa digabungkan dengan elemen lain seperti auxiliary verbs, dan auxiliary verbs itulah yang nantinya akan dikonjugasikan.
